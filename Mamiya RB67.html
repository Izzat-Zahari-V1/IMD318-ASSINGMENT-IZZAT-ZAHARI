What is It? Picture for Reference

The Mamiya RB67 Pro, or the variant that I use; Pro-S is a medium format Single Lens Reflex (SLR) analoge camera. Famous for its modular desing, allowing the user to almost change evry aspect of the camera from the format to the viewfinder. It also allows the user to change film on the go rather than draining it first with the use of darkside, which can be stored on the side of the camera when not in usde. This camera is also a Medium Format, shoots in the 6X7 ratio.

This is my personal Mamiya RB67 Pro-S, alongside it with the prism finder and the two lens for it.

The camera itself have proof to be a reliable, espeically when shooting expensive medium format it is to mention that this camera itself, without the prism attachemnet is can get as heavy as 2KG without film. It strains the hand and the neck, but it worth every second of it

This camera did not have a build in meter, which I rely on external metering service such as Ashtori shoe meter
