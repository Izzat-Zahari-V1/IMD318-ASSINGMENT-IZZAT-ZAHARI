What is it? Image reference

The Canon F-1, especially the 1971 variant that the user use is a 35MM Single Lens Reflex (SLR) professional camera introduce by Canon. The F1 is famous, espeially withink the Malaysian Photoggraphy circles during its hay days to its ability to interchnage parts of the camera ranging from its film back to the prism. The F1 also comes with a lot of the accecories expected of a Professional camera such as an auto winder, a device that can be attached to the camera that allow it to be winded to the next frame automatically. The F1's fully manual controlled, alongside it's mechanical mechanism and heavy duty build are well known. It is known its inferior to the its other variant, it is consider a great and favorite camera's among profassional and now, enthusiastc alike.

On the left is My personal F1, ALbeit suffering from a Dead Capacitor Syndrome due to its age and possible misrepair from the previous owner Alongside it, a few lens such as the 28mm Super Takumar and the infamous Helios 44-2

The camera itself is a good camera, howvere it is quite loud, due to the early variant's infamous "ping" where the shutter itself produce the sound. Some user of this variant find it quite anoying but i personally like it. The shutter itself on my speciment is accurate and relaible.
